    Fly into DIA.

    Cheapest tickets are generally Frontier as it's based in Denver.

    RTD Bus Station

    1400 WALNUT ST, BOULDER, CO 80302, USA

    (303) 413-7300

    Use the RTD bus AB1 to get from the airport right to downtown hotels. It's the easiest and cheapest option that we take when we travel.

    We also use it all the time to get around town! Our house is using bus 204 or the Skip from downtown to North Boulder.

    Download the RTD app on your phone, buy tickets on it, and when you activate that ticket you simply scan a generated QR code when you enter the bus.

    You can also use Lyft or Uber to get around!

    We have local family and friends and can try to help arrange carpools for any events, so let us know.

    Things to Do in Boulder

    Pearl St. Mall

    1303 PEARL ST, BOULDER, CO 80302, USA

    Look at cute shops and find food at the Pearl St. walking mall!

    Our ceremony and reception will be near this pedestrian walking mall, which we'll stroll down together! For our afterparty we plan to wander over and crawl to some of the bars.

    CHAUTAUQUA PARK

    BASELINE RD & 9TH ST, BOULDER, CO 80302, USA

    Go hiking or listen to live music in an old wooden amphitheater! The Bluebell trail is a nice chill hike, the Royal Arch trail is a sweaty reward!

    ELDORADO CANYON STATE PARK

    9 KNEALE RD, ELDORADO SPRINGS, CO 80025, USA

    Go hike and watch some trad climbing up in Eldo! The Continental Divide Overlook trail is a pretty and moderate trail, and the Fowler trail has spyglasses to help see the climbers!

    NCAR TRAIL HEAD

    1850 TABLE MESA DR, BOULDER, CO 80305, USA

    National Center for Atmospheric Research Labs has a great building with a weather walk (plaques describing pressure systems!) and more trails behind--including where Connor proposed to Alice!
